AboutToronto, CA


  

    

      
      
Ivan Karaulov is a Toronto-based cinematographer and gaffer, born in
      Volgograd, Russia. He started at 13 with BMX videos and in his early 20s was shooting
      music videos for artists like Pharaoh and Slava KPSS. He lived and worked
      in Moscow before relocating to Toronto in 2018.

      
He's worked as a gaffer and key grip on every kind of set — from
      one-man-band shoots to tier-A union productions, including projects
      selected for TIFF and Sundance. That range made him fast and adaptable:
      the person who finds a solution whatever the budget, crew, or location
      allows.

      
As a director of photography he takes his cues from music, 80s cinema,
      the luminous seas of Aivazovsky and the color of Roerich, and the ordinary
      light he notices on the street.
